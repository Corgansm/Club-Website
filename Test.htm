Help

Am I allowed to share my account with someone else?

— No, your account is HWID locked when we setup a loader. This means that the cheat will only work on your device, and if you change it you can request a HWID reset.

Which operating systems do you support?

— We only support Windows 10 for all of our products.

Are your cheats safe to use?

— Our cheats are usually safe, but there is a risk that game/anti-cheat developers may be able to detect the cheat in the future. Every cheat can get detected, but it is unlikely to happen. You can check the detection status of the cheat on our forum or in the loader. If our cheat gets detected we will inform you right away and we'll find a way to bypass the anti-cheat.

How long does it take to get access to the cheat after I purchase a subscription?

— You sadly wont have access to the cheat imidiatly, we will have to manually add you to the database because our website isn't working right now

What happens if the game gets an update and the cheat doesn't work anymore?

— Our cheats should work fine after game updates, but if there's a big update and something is broken, our developers will fix it as soon as possible.

Is there a free trial available?

— No, you need to purchase a subscription to get access to the cheat.
